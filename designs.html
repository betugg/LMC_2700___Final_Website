About Me, The Drawer of The Stuff on This Page


I am very passionate about what I do!

I have always had an incredible passion and love for animation. It has been a part of my life ever since I was little. Whether it be Disney, Dreamworks, Studio Ghibli, or any other studio, animation has been an incredible art form in my life that I have admired forever and always will admire. I have wanted to be an animator to create the worlds of my imagination and others. This is why I am so passionate about learning as well, the more I learn, the more I know about the world and the people in it, which is so important when it comes to animation. I want everyone's stories to be told, so I will do my best to have the skills to do this.
